How can organizations create their account?

Non-Government Organizations who are interested can register for free and use the app in promoting their projects. They can simply visit the Sign Up page to create their account. Click User Icon (upper right) > Sign Up

What are the requirements to register for an account?

Aside from the organization's email address, the app requires them to include their certifications and pictures of documents that would prove the organization's legitimacy.
